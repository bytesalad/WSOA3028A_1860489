Johnathan Mbeki Contact Information

It is the year 1955 and Johnathan lives in Sophiatown, Johannesburg, South Africa.

He can be found at the neighborhood church playing music alongside his bandmates, at his house closeby where he stays with his mom, or at the town shibeen playing the upbeat grooves of western composers Duke Ellington and Louis Armstrong or the distinctly South African flavours of Hugh Masekela and Miriam Makeba.

Instruments (Play):

Contrabass, Piano

Instruments (Compose for):

Contrabass, Piano, Drum Set, Woodwind, Brass

Genre:

Gospel, New Orleans/Dixieland Jazz, Hardbop, Ballad, Blues, South African Jazz
